Ultrasound-based Distance Measurement System



  I developed an ultrasound-based distance measurement system using the HC-SR04 sensor to measure object distances ranging from 5 cm to 200 cm.

  I programmed the ATmega328P MCU on an Arduino Uno in Embedded C to calculate distances based on the time taken by ultrasound waves to bounce back from objects.

  The system featured a 0.96" OLED display with a resolution of 128x64 pixels to show distance measurements in both centimeters and inches, achieving an accuracy of 1 cm.
